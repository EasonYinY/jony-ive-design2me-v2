# 艾维设计视觉语料库

> **版本**：1.0
> **日期**：2026-06-19
> **目的**：通过产品图像反推设计思维，实现跨案例交叉验证
> **方法**：同一产品多角度图像 → 提取设计特征 → 反推设计决策

## 二、核心案例：多角度图像提示词

### 案例1: BALMUDA Sailing Lantern (2025)

#### 2.1.1 Hero View（整体关系）

**图像提示词**：
```
A portable maritime lantern, photorealistic, soft studio lighting,
hand-held view, showing the lantern in human context,
demonstrating the relationship between person and light source,
brass bezel, woven fabric lanyard for carrying, clear glass dome,
Fresnel lens interior, single control knob on top,
the form language suggests "carrying" and "hanging" rituals,
not a desk lamp, warm ambient glow from LED source,
maritime archetype but made with contemporary materials,
not nostalgic replica, trust through visible craftsmanship,
the overall form speaks to portability and outdoor use,
the design prioritizes function: illumination that can be extinguished, 
carried, and hung in various environments.
```

**关系命题**：人与光、携带、熄灭、海洋环境的关系

**反推思维**：
- 形态不是"好看的灯"，而是"可以被提着走的仪式光源"
- 为什么要织带？→ carry 和 hang 行为
- 为什么要单旋钮？→ extinguish 和 tune 统一控制
- 为什么要玻璃圆顶？→ 航海记忆 + 可见光源

---

#### 2.1.2 Side Proportion（比例分析）

**图像提示词**：
```
Side profile of a portable lantern, technical drawing style,
showing proportions: height to width ratio approximately 1.5:1,
glass dome diameter nearly equal to base diameter,
single control knob sits precisely at center axis,
woven lanyard attachment points symmetrically placed,
brass protective cage frames glass dome without overwhelming,
the form demonstrates balance between protection and transparency,
Fresnel lens creates characteristic curved light refraction,
no unnecessary protrusions, every element earns its place,
precision-ground glass for even light diffusion,
metal base provides weight for stability when hung,
the proportional logic suggests careful consideration of 
how hands will grip, how it will hang, how it will rest.
```

**比例关系反推**：
- 高度≈宽度的1.5倍 → 便于握持和悬挂
- 玻璃罩直径≈底座直径 → 平衡的视觉关系
- 旋钮位于中心轴 → 单一控制点的空间逻辑

---

#### 2.1.3 Macro Tactile（触觉节点）

**图像提示词**：
```
Close-up macro photograph of lantern control mechanism,
single precision-machined knob at top, brushed metal finish,
rotational detents provide tactile feedback for brightness levels,
the knob serves dual function: brightness and color temperature,
and extinguishing - one gesture for multiple behaviors,
visible screw heads at base indicate repairability,
metal cage bars provide grip texture for carrying,
the tactile nodes answer questions: where do my fingers confirm state?
the knob position allows one-handed operation,
woven fabric lanyard provides grip without cutting circulation,
embossed logo on brass bezel provides brand confirmation without decoration,
every touch point designed for long-term comfort and reliability.
```

**触觉节点分析**：
| 触觉节点 | 承担行为 | 反馈形式 |
|----------|----------|----------|
| 旋钮 | 亮度+色温+熄灭 | 阻尼段落感 |
| 金属护架 | 握持+携带 | 金属冷感+纹理 |
| 织带 | 悬挂+背负 | 织物柔韧 |
| 底座螺丝 | 打开维修 | 可见性→信任 |

---

#### 2.1.4 Material Close-up（材料工艺）

**图像提示词**：
```
Material detail shot of lantern construction,
precision-ground Fresnel lens for light diffusion,
not "premium glass" but glass that performs optical function,
brushed stainless steel cage, visible grain direction,
the metal aging gracefully with use, scratches become character,
woven lanyard in maritime-appropriate textile,
the materials answer: what function does each material serve?
what emotional quality does each material communicate?
what happens to this material over ten years?
fresnel lens for even light distribution,
LED for energy efficiency and warmth,
stainless steel for salt-water resistance,
the material honesty approach: every surface serves a purpose,
no materials applied purely for "luxury" appearance.
```

**材料职责分析**：
```
precision-ground Fresnel lens  →  for light diffusion (功能)
                               →  for maritime memory (情绪)
                               →  for even illumination (技术)

brushed stainless steel        →  for salt corrosion resistance (功能)
                               →  for aging gracefully (时间)
                               →  for maritime heritage (情绪)

woven textile lanyard          →  for carrying and hanging (行为)
                               →  for grip comfort (触觉)
                               →  for replaceability (维护)
```

---

#### 2.1.5 Use-state（场景关系）

**图像提示词**：
```
Lifestyle photograph showing lantern in maritime context,
hung from boat rigging in evening light,
the lantern becomes part of the maritime ritual,
not decoration but functional lighting for navigation,
the context demonstrates: this is not a desk lamp,
this is light you carry, hang, and extinguish,
the relationship between human, lantern, and sea environment,
the warm light against dark water creates emotional resonance,
the lantern designed to accompany voyages, not just illuminate,
the form language answers: how does this object exist in real life?
it hangs, it swings with the boat, it lights the way home.
```

**场景关系反推**：
- 为什么设计成可以悬挂？→ 航海环境需要
- 为什么强调熄灭仪式？→ 航海灯的信号意义
- 为什么用暖光？→ 海洋夜晚的情感需求
